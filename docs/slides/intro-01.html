Basic Probability Notation

  • Sample space: \(\Omega\)
  • Event: \(A \subseteq \Omega\)
  • Probability of \(A\): \(\Prob(A)\)
  • Complement: \(A^{c}\)
  • Conditional probability: \[ \Prob(A \mid B) = \frac{\Prob(A \cap B)}{\Prob(B)}, \qquad \Prob(B) > 0 \]
  • Expectation \(\Ex[Y]=\)

Key idea: probabilities are always between \(0\) and \(1\).
